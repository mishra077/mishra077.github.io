Hey 👋, thanks for stopping by!

I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI capabilities, focusing on developing efficient and interpretable deep learning models. I am committed to bridging the gap between theoretical advancements and real-world applications in AI. 

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from the [University of Southern California, Viterbi School of Engineering](https://www.cs.usc.edu/), and a B.Tech in [Computer and Communication Engineering from Manipal University Jaipur](https://jaipur.manipal.edu/foe/department-of-computer-communicarion-engineering.php).

My research has been published in journals such as IEEE Transactions on Image Processing and IEEE Signal Processing Letters. I have gained valuable research experience as a Deep Learning Intern at [AiBEE Inc.](https://www.aibee.com/), a Research Intern at [iLab USC](http://ilab.usc.edu/itti/), and [Vision Intelligence Lab](https://visionintelligence.github.io/), MNIT Jaipur, working on projects like human Re-Identification pipelines, object tracking, and motion segmentation.

Professionally, I served as a Software Engineer at [Cansera](https://www.cansera.com/), where I designed an interactive diagnostic web app for oncologists and engineered informative dashboards for cancer patients.

Key projects in my career include implementing GPT-2 from scratch, engineering components of the Stable Diffusion pipeline, and developing MLOps batch-serving architecture. I am always eager to connect with fellow AI enthusiasts and researchers. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss AI, machine learning, or potential collaborations!
